Whale analysis

Using our data across all recorded institutional positions, you can view the highest changes in current positions (whether institutions have increased or decreased their holdings), and also view the largest changes of new or sold-out positions. These datasets act as a measure of confidence within large institutions and hedge funds. Insider datasets have also been collected in order to highlight the most recent trades taken by corporate executives and large company shareholders, along with visualising trade actions across the stock price timeline. For this, simply search for any stock in the search bar
