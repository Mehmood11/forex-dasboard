Government trading tracker

Since the approval of the Stop Trading on Congressional Knowledge Act of 2012 (STOCK Act), US government officials are required to make public of all securities transactions with a value above $1,000 within 30 days of receiving notice of transaction and within 45 days of the transaction date. We collect this data through reputable sources (eFD) and process into a readable database. You can view this information below.
